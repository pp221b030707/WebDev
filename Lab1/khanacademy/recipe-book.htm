Recipe #1:Rabandas
  • Time: 25 mins
  • Serves: 6 servings
Ingredients Quantity
milk 3.5 cups
granuled sugar 0.25 of a cup
honey 2 tbsp.
baguette 1
eggs 5
cinnamon sugar 1 cup

Steps required:

Dip the bread in warm milk, then in egg.Toasts are shallow fied in oil,tossed in cinnamon sugar,served with honey.

Source: Rabandas recipe

Recipe #2:Avocado toasts
  • Time: 15 mins
  • Serves: 2 servings
Ingredients Quantity
eggs 1
salt and pepper a pinch
avocado 1

Steps required:

Boil the egg, cut it in 2 halfs.
Cut the bread in 2 slices.
Cut the avocado in half, slice both halfs thinly.
Toast the bread
Put avocado and egg onto the bread slices

Recipe #3:Scrambled eggs
  • Time: 15
  • Serves: 1 servings
Ingredients Quantity
eggs 1
salt and pepper a pinch
milk 1 cup

Steps required:

Mix the egg and the milk together.
Put salt and pepper
Put the mixture into the pan and mix till it's cooked
